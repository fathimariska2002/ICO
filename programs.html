Flagship Program
Webinar Series

Global Voices of the Displaced

This flagship webinar series amplifies the experiences, challenges, and aspirations of displaced communities around the world. It creates a vital platform for dialogue between those most affected and those with the power to create change.

Objectives

  • Provide a platform for refugees, migrants, and displaced persons
  • Facilitate dialogue between policymakers, experts, and affected communities
  • Highlight humanitarian needs and policy gaps
  • Promote inclusive, rights-based solutions
