Our Story

History of ICO

The International Congress Organization (ICO) was established as an international non-profit association (AISBL) under Belgian law, with its registered office in Brussels.

Founded to promote global cooperation and humanitarian values, ICO has grown into a platform for international dialogue, capacity building, and community empowerment.

Its statutes outline a clear mission: to support vulnerable communities, foster peace, and strengthen global partnerships through education, congresses, and humanitarian initiatives.

Secretariat & Governance

ICO operates under a transparent and accountable governance framework defined by its statutes.

Supreme Decision-Making Body

  • Admission/exclusion of members
  • Appointment of ambassadors
