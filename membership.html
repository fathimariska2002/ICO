Membership & Partners

Become part of a global community committed to justice, peace, and human rights.

Requirements

Eligibility Criteria

International organisations seeking Supporting Membership must meet the following requirements.

A

Legal Status & International Scope

  • Formally registered non-profit entity
  • Minimum two years of continuous operation
  • Good legal standing — no pending sanctions
B

Alignment with ICO's Mission

  • Promoting dialogue, justice, human rights, peace
  • Supporting vulnerable or marginalised communities
  • Advancing international cooperation
  • Contributing to humanitarian action
C

Governance & Accountability

  • Transparent governance structure
  • Sound financial management
  • Non-discrimination principles
  • No affiliation with armed groups
D

Commitment to ICO Partnership

  • Formally commit to supporting ICO's mission
  • Participate in ICO events and initiatives
  • Comply with ICO's Statutes and Code of Conduct
How to Apply

Application Process

1

Prepare Documents

Gather registration certificate, governing documents, annual report, financial statements, and statement of alignment with ICO's objectives.

2

Submit Application

Submit in English or French to the ICO Executive Secretariat. ICO acknowledges receipt within 10 working days.

3

Board Review

The Board of Directors reviews your application. A final decision is issued within 30 days. An interview may be requested.

4

Decision & Onboarding

Board decisions are final at first instance, with the right of appeal to the General Assembly. Approved members join the ICO network.
